Sheryl Lee Ralph: Our Dream Girl

In this episode of the Kelly Ripa Podcast, host Kelly Ripa sits down with the incredibly talented and inspiring Sheryl Lee Ralph. Known for her exceptional career as a singer, actress, and activist, Sheryl Lee Ralph opens up about her incredible journey in the entertainment industry.

Sheryl Lee Ralph shares her experiences, starting from her early days as a singer for the renowned group Penthouse, to her collaborations with legendary actor Sidney Poitier. Through their heartfelt conversation, Kelly and Sheryl discuss the highs and lows of Sheryl’s career and the lessons she’s learned along the way.

One of the highlights of this episode is Sheryl’s discussion about her electrifying performance at the Super Bowl. She also shares the joyous news of her son’s engagement and dives into her collaboration with the multi-talented Rihanna.

“Being a part of the Super Bowl halftime show was a dream come true. The energy and excitement on that stage were indescribable. It’s a moment I will cherish forever.”

Through this insightful conversation, listeners gain a deeper understanding of Sheryl Lee Ralph’s personal and professional life. Her determination, resilience, and talent have made her a true icon in the entertainment industry.
